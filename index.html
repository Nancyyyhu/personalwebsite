About

Growing up in a family shaped by the fusion of a business-minded father and an artistically sensitive mother, I have always searched for a balance between rationality and intuition, commerce and art.

I rarely call myself an “artist,” but rather a “designer,” because I believe design should carry meaning. For me, communication is never only about visual expression; it is about constructing connections, values, and systems of understanding.

Experience

I graduated from Parsons School of Design, majoring in Communication Design with a minor in Marketing and Management.

I have worked with companies including Ogilvy and Dongfeng Peugeot in France, and interned at the art department of Orient Group in both Beijing and Shanghai, as well as at the design studio “Randerland.”

I also co-founded a sustainable fashion brand “R'cizze,” through which we organized design competitions and fashion shows in both New York and Shanghai.
