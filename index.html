About

Believe in design driven by meaning and intention rather than aesthetic expression alone. I am a designer who values purpose, clarity, and outcome—approaching communication design as a way to construct ideas, connections, and systems of expressing. Working across disciplines and media, I love to explore how different formats, materials, and visual languages can intersect to create designs that is contextual, impactful and sustainable.

Experience

Graduated from Parsons School of Design with a major in Communication Design and a minor in Marketing and Management. I Worked with globally recognized organizations including Ogilvy and Dongfeng Peugeot, and gained experience through internships at the art department of Emeraldscape Cultural Company in Beijing and the design studio Randerland in Shanghai.Also, I co-founded the sustainable fashion brand R'cizze and established an online platform through which my team organized design competitions and fashion shows in both New York and Shanghai.
